Traffic Recer

A fun and exciting car racing game, where you drive your car
at crazy speed through highways crowded with other cars. Your goal is to overtake as many cars as possible
without crashing into them, and collect points and money to buy new cars and upgrade your current car

  • Variety of Cars:
    There is a wide range of cars to choose from, which vary in performance, speed and power.


  • Car Upgrade
    Immerse yourself in an exciting story full of mysterious events.


  • Multiple game modes:
    The game offers various game modes, such as races against time and races against other players..


  • Realistic graphics:
    The game features realistic 3D graphics, which increases the fun of playing.


  • Easy Control:
    The game features easy and simple controls, making it suitable for all age groups.
